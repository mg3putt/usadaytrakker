Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: USA Day Trakker collects no personal data whatsoever. Everything stays on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no tracking, and no analytics of any kind.

Information We Collect

None.

USA Day Trakker does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no registration process, and no backend servers.

Data Storage

All data you enter into the app — including your trip dates and notes — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

Third-Party Services

USA Day Trakker does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. No third-party code of any kind is included in the app.

Internet Access

USA Day Trakker does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

USA Day Trakker does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Apple Platform Data

As an app distributed through the Apple App Store, Apple may collect certain usage and diagnostic data in accordance with their own privacy policy. This data is collected by Apple, not by USA Day Trakker, and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Given the nature of the app, we do not anticipate any changes that would involve collecting user data.
